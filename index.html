The Fire & Slice History

More Than 20+ years of experience

Fire & Slice is a vibrant, modern pizza restaurant that brings the art of wood-fired cooking to the heart of the city. Known for its rustic yet contemporary atmosphere, the restaurant offers an open kitchen where guests can watch skilled pizzaiolos craft delicious pizzas from scratch. Using fresh, locally sourced ingredients and high-quality cheeses, Fire & Slice serves up a diverse menu of traditional and innovative pizzas. The signature wood-fired oven infuses each pizza with a smoky, crispy crust, while toppings range from classic margherita to gourmet combinations like truffle mushroom and prosciutto.


In addition to its mouth-watering pizzas, Fire & Slice is a community hub, where friends and families gather to enjoy great food and a welcoming atmosphere.
